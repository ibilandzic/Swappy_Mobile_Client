What is Swappy?

Swappy is an application for exchanging or giving away your old clothes. It's super easy to use: simply register and explore posted ads.

Can I mark item as favorite?

Of course! You can mark unlimited items as your favorite but make sure you are registered ;)

How can I post ad?

Posting ads is simple. Go to "New ad", fill in the form and submit the data. That's it!

How much do I have to pay?

Swappy is free of charge. We don't take any fee nor we charge shipping. Shipping is arranged between users.
